Why

Why Shift Exists

Shift is not (just) a conference; Shift is Infobip’s strategic credibility platform in the global technology ecosystem.

Its purpose is to:

  • Position Infobip as a serious, AI-first technology company
  • Build long-term trust and strengthen Infobip’s perception with technology and product leadership of potential buyers and partners
  • Create relationship capital that enables enterprise growth over time

Shift is not a lead-generation event; it is a brand and relationship accelerator.

Context

Market Context & Positioning

As companies scale, their expectations from technology platforms evolve — from speed and experimentation to reliability, security, and trusted infrastructure.

With maturity, perception shifts accordingly: enterprise buyers choose platforms that signal stability, resilience, and long-term partnership.

For Infobip to move upmarket, perception must evolve beyond a transactional CPaaS vendor toward:

  • A reliable enterprise-grade platform
  • An AI-enabled communications infrastructure layer
  • A strategic partner for global digital systems

Shift plays a critical role in accelerating that perception shift. The conference must increasingly align with senior engineering and technology leaders who influence large-scale platform decisions.
